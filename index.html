About Me

Hi! I’m Nima, a computational materials scientist who loves exploring materials at the atomic scale. I work with molecular dynamics, DFT, and machine learning to study plasma-surface interactions and material behavior. Python is my go-to for crunching data and building efficient, reproducible workflows.
